About Us

Our Weather App is designed to provide real-time weather updates for cities around the world. Whether you're planning a trip or simply want to know the current weather conditions, our app has you covered.

We use data from trusted weather APIs to give you accurate and up-to-date information on temperature, humidity, wind speed, and more.

Our mission is to provide a simple, intuitive interface that helps users make informed decisions based on current weather conditions.
